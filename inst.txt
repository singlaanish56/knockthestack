INSTRUCTIONS
You have to recover the gold hidden at the bottom of the stack by beating the computer.
In your turn , the stack is decreased by the score that you have achieved
and increased in the computer's turn by its score. You could challenge the computer by spending 10 gold coins
such that if your score is more than computer score the square of your score gets subtracted , else if it is less than computer score 
then square of computer score gets added. If score is same then challenge is nullified.
